The Consultant’s Precision: Brubaker Quality Control

At the top of the professional stack is Brubaker Quality Control (BQC), the management intervention for organizations ready to face the "Uncomfortable truth" of their operational failures. The Director’s Eye: Kevin Deon

In the film world, he is Kevin Deon. As an award-winning director, writer, and actor with MGM associations, he brings the "Director's Eye" to business diagnostics. Having founded the LVFTA in Las Vegas, he builds infrastructure for artists rather than just participating. His training in the Stanislavski and Meisner methods allows him to read people and rooms with unusual precision.

The Lyricist’s Soul: Tulsa Flood

Tulsa Flood is where the blues, rock, and narrative force converge. Fronting the band psychedelic blues band Rhythm Affair since 2018, his sound is rooted in his love of great blues legends like Peter Green and Muddy Waters. Mentored by the legendary patriarch of the Jackson Family Joe Jackson and having also worked with music legends like Charo and Prince, this is the emotional proof of the force visible in all of his endeavors, a refusal to tolerate mediocrity.
