KEVIN DEON NORTON

Consultant • Filmmaker • Musician

Brubaker Quality Control

Kevin Deon Norton is the force behind Brubaker Quality Control (BQC), where he serves as a high-impact management consultant specializing in leadership development and business efficiency. With a track record that includes global giants like Costco and 3M, Kevin identifies the "Babysitting Tax" and "Internal Blindness" that quietly bleed company profits.

Beyond the boardroom, he is a multi-hyphenate talent known in the film industry as Kevin Deon—an award-winning director and actor with credits tied to MGM and Warner Bros—and in the music world as Tulsa Flood, mentored by the legendary Joe Jackson. His "Director’s Eye" allows him to read human behavior with precision, whether he’s fixing a broken corporate culture or directing a high-stakes scene.
