Kevin Deon Norton

Entrepreneur. Entertainer. Motivational Speaker. Management Consultant.
In an era of hyper-specialization, Kevin Deon Norton is the living refutation of the myth that you can only master one thing. Whether he is directing a film, composing a blues anthem, or diagnosing a corporate profit leak, he understands that the fundamental currency is always the same: Human Capital.

The Director’s Eye

Known as Kevin Deon in the film world, he is a prolific director and actor who brings cinematic discipline to organizational chaos. He founded the LVFTA to mentor artists in authentic psychological truth.

The Lyricist’s Soul

As Tulsa Flood, the gravel-voiced frontman of Rhythm Affair, he weaves psychedelic blues into raw narrative tapestries—using the rhythm of humanity to build trust and influence.

The HR Architect

Through Brubaker Quality Control, Kevin identifies the "Internal Blindness" and "Babysitting Tax" that bleeds companies dry, providing the direct intervention required to reclaim potential.
