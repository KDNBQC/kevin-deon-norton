The Architect of Performance

The Cinematic Craftsman

Known in the industry as Kevin Deon, he is an award-winning filmmaker and casting director (MGM, Warner Bros). He founded the LVFTA to mentor artists in the authentic Stanislavski and Meisner methods.

The Musical Nomad

As Tulsa Flood, he leads the psychedelic blues-rock outfit Rhythm Affair. His artistry was forged through a three-year mentorship under Joe Jackson and collaborations with icons like Prince and Charo.
