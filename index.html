Kevin Deon Norton

Consultant | Filmmaker | Musician

In an era that rewards narrow lanes, Kevin Deon Norton stands as a true multi-hyphenate. He doesn't just dabble; he builds, leads, and diagnoses the truth in systems and people. Whether in the boardroom, on set, or on stage, his instinct is the same: eliminate the noise and amplify the signal.

The Consultant’s Precision: Brubaker Quality Control

At the core of his professional life is Brubaker Quality Control (BQC). Kevin identifies the "Babysitting Tax"—the high cost of paying managers to supervise instead of lead. Having worked with giants like Costco and 3M, he targets the "Internal Blindness" that allows mediocrity to drain profits. He doesn’t offer DIY guides; he offers high-level intervention for companies with over six employees striving for perfection.

The Director’s Eye: Kevin Deon

In the cinematic world, he is Kevin Deon. An award-winning director and actor with MGM credits, he founded the Las Vegas Film and Television Artists (LVFTA), growing it to over 800 members. Trained in the Stanislavski and Meisner methods, his "Director’s Eye" allows him to read human behavior with a precision that translates directly from the film set to corporate strategy.

The Lyricist’s Soul: Tulsa Flood

Performing as Tulsa Flood, he is the frontman for Rhythm Affair. His music is a "precious artifact" of psychedelic blues and rock. Shaped by a three-year mentorship as personal assistant to the late Joe Jackson and professional experience with Prince, his artistry is rooted in the same discipline and search for authentic truth found in his consulting work.
